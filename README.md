# 🌾 કૃષિસેતુ - AI Powered Farmer Marketplace

## 📖 Project Overview

**કૃષિસેતુ** (KrishiSetu) is a revolutionary AI-powered marketplace that directly connects farmers with consumers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for agricultural products. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with traditional farming wisdom to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem.

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission

### 🌟 Our Vision
To create a world where every farmer has direct access to markets, fair prices, and modern agricultural knowledge, empowering rural communities and transforming agriculture into a sustainable, profitable, and respected profession.

### 🎯 Our Mission
- **🤝 Direct Connection**: Bridge the gap between farmers and consumers
- **💰 Fair Pricing**: Eliminate middlemen and ensure farmers get their deserved income
- **🤖 AI Empowerment**: Provide 24/7 AI assistance for farming queries
- **🌱 Sustainability**: Promote sustainable and organic farming practices
- **📚 Knowledge Sharing**: Democratize agricultural information and best practices
- **📱 Digital Inclusion**: Bring modern technology to rural farming communities
